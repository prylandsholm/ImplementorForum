Why OBI4x?

A common digital language starts here

This mock-up positions OBI4x as a practical home for organizations that want to align data, tools, ontologies and implementation methods across the lifecycle of industrial assets, products and systems.

Reduce interpretation gaps

Replace manual interpretation and custom mappings with shared semantics and reusable patterns.

Enable AI and automation

Prepare data for machine interpretation, validation, reasoning and smarter industrial workflows.

Support digital continuity

Connect engineering, operations, maintenance and digital twin use cases using a common backbone.

Scale cross-company collaboration

Build trust and clarity across operators, suppliers, EPCs, software providers and standards bodies.

About the forum

From summit announcement to implementation community

The forum is presented here as an industry collaboration space for sharing implementation experience, curating resources and implementation guides, and aligning how ontology-based interoperability is applied in practice.

The site concept follows the pattern of an implementor-forum website: a clear value proposition, visible use cases, a roadmap, events, working groups and a practical resource hub.
