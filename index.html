We would like to thank all contributors for making the Industrial Semantic Interoperability Summit 2026 a great success. With around 150 participants from industry, associations, and academia, the event demonstrated strong interest and momentum around ontology-based interoperability and the ISO 23726 Ontology based interoperability (OBI) standard series.

The recordings below capture key insights and practical experiences shared during the summit. We encourage you to explore the material and join the emerging semantic4iop Implementor Forum.
